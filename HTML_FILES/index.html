The Young Designer Awards will be inviting applications from young professionals, students and freelancers under the age of 30 years who will be the future champions. While discovering and nurturing the next generation of design talent, the award will help the design professionals develop their skills through cross-learning opportunities and networking with key industry players in the domain.

As an early indicator of success for the products in the concept stage or the products which are yet to be launched in the market, this award lets young designers to register and patent winning products, systems and services for commercial value generation.
